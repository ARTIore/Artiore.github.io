# ARTIore Integration Playbook

## Why this matters
Organizations need clear operating boundaries so AI improves intake and clarity without replacing professional judgment.

## Quick summary
- ARTIore supports intake, structure, and preparation.
- Professionals retain final decision authority.
- Privacy, escalation, and quality loops are mandatory.

## Eligible organizational contexts
- Schools
- Clinics
- NGOs
- Community legal/service desks
- Workforce and HR-adjacent support systems

## Professional-Ready Output Standard: The ARTIore Brief
One-page handoff format:
1. Goal
2. Context (3 bullets)
3. Timeline and deadlines
4. What user already tried
5. Top 5 provider questions
6. Constraints (money/time/language/transport)

## Integration rules
- ARTIore does intake + clarity.
- Humans make final decisions and accountability calls.
- Keep quality logs with privacy protections.
- Escalate high-risk or ambiguous cases rapidly.

## Accessibility and inclusion requirements
- Low literacy mode (simple language, shorter outputs)
- Disability support (screen-reader structure, text alternatives)
- Language support (bilingual summary + key term translation)
- Low connectivity fallback (SMS-length summary + printable one-page output)

## Continuous improvement process
- Feedback categories: error, unclear, unsafe, biased, missing resource
- Public changelog: what changed, why, what stayed stable
- Regression checks: safety, clarity, complexity layering, uncertainty labels
- Periodic community audits including external critics

## Implementation rollout checklist
- Define escalation matrix and owner roles.
- Train staff on ARTIore Brief handoff.
- Establish privacy retention and access policy.
- Pilot with limited scope and weekly review.
- Expand only after safety and quality thresholds are met.
